### 🏠 Home Screen 

The home screen includes:
- **Banner** – Displays current offers and discounts
- **Categories** – Browse products by Men, Women, Shoes, and Accessories
- **New Arrivals** – Shows the latest products with name and price
- **Bottom Navigation** – Quick access to Home, Wishlist, Cart, and Profile
